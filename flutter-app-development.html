FAQs

Can Flutter be used to develop apps for both iOS and Android platforms?
Yes. Flutter is a cross-platform language that enables app entrepreneurs to build mobile apps for both iOS and Android using the same source code, and that too with a truly native feel. By choosing Flutter as your app development framework and hiring expert Flutter developers, you can get both iOS and Android versions of your mobile app within a tight budget and deadlines.
What types of apps can be developed with Flutter?
From fascinating 2d games to on-demand mobile applications, there are over 500,000 apps made using Flutter, including some prominent names like eBay, Alibaba, Dream 11, BMW, Google Pay, etc. You can also migrate your existing app to flutter to leverage this feature-rich platform and build an iOS or Android version of your app with the same source code. As a leading Flutter app development company, TekRevol can help you build a cross-platform app using Flutter for both iOS and Android and convert your existing app to Flutter. Connect with us for a free discovery session to learn how you can scale your app business using Flutter.
How long does it take to develop a Flutter app?
The time to build a Flutter app depends primarily on the app's complexity and the Flutter development company you're partnering with. On average, it takes 3 – 6 months to build a fully functioning app using Flutter. However, the development time is proportional to the complex features in your app and the number of developers and designers working on your project.
What is the cost of developing a Flutter app?
The cost of flutter app development services is comparatively lower to native iOS or Android app development. However, it's impossible to determine the exact cost of an app without conducting consultation sessions with stakeholders and clearly understanding their project needs.
